About us

It's an open secret that dating sites suck. They do so because interests of their users contradict interests of their investors: the more users they match, the less users they have. We are going to add some fresh water to this stale pond and do the unthinkable: put interests of our users first. We will do that for fun and we can do so because we are not beholden to third party investors.

Our site is open to all corporate employees in the US and only to them, because we believe that a shared background is necessary for an honest dating experience. We do not employ traditional predatory techniques such as creating the illusion of abundance, supplementing the shallow userbase with bots and gatekeeping communication between users who liked each other. However, running a dating site isn't free, so beyond a basic free tier we offer, you'll need to pay a small fee to be seen by others with mutually matching preferences. The good news is that we verify all accounts, so each mutual match is real and is likely to result in interest from both parties. We do not gatekeep communication: once a match is found, you're encouraged to continue the conversation elsewhere.
